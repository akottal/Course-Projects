Xinu scheduling policy:

Advantages:

Simple and Quick
No additional calculations are required while sccheduling.

Disadvantages:

Starvation is possible.


Linux like scheduling policy:

Advantages:

Fair share of CPU given to each process based on the priority.
No risk of starvation.

Disadvantages:

Complex calculations are involved while calculating goodness and quantum for the processes.

Multi queue scheduling process:

Advantages:

Fair share of CPU given to each process based on the priority, and also real time processes and normal processes are treated differently.
No risk of starvation.

Disadvantages:

Along with the calculations for goodness and quantum, switching between the queues is an additional overhead.
